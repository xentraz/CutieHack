About US

We are a small company founded on the idea that isolation shouldn't be something you dread. We want isloation become a more social, physcial and mentally healthy part of our new everyday lives. Beacuse of this we have created a website where you can become part of an amazing community of people there to help each other through isolation. We have a team of professional health care workers including therapists, dietisians, personal trainers etc. that can help you get through these difficult times in a more healthy and social manner.
