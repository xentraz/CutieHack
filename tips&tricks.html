Tips and Tricks

Workout tricks

Bodyweight excersizes are a great way to start off! Push ups, sit ups, squats and planks are a great way to work out and build up your core!

You could always use jugs of water as a weight, though be careful to use something that has an even weight.

Now that you're stuck inside, try to get out! Take a walk or a jog out in the nature! It's good for both body and mind!

Mentality Tricks

Don't sit inside all day and stare at the screen, do something! Learn something new and get physically active.

Remember to check up on your family, give them a call! Make sure to take care of those close to you.

You got more time now, why not use it to learn how to cook better? Eat healthier and better. You'd be surprised how much it helps.

Social Tricks

Try joining an online forum, like this one! You'll meet allot of new people with likeminded interests.

Have you tried playing online games? There's allot of people out there, some more interesting than others.

Why not go on a trip into the forrest with a friend? Be sure to keep your distanse though!
